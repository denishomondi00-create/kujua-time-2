# Kujua Time Product Blueprint

This repo follows the owner-supplied product blueprint for Kujua Time.

## One-line definition

Kujua Time is a branded scheduling and client workflow platform for service businesses that helps them get booked, get paid, stay organized, and automate follow-up from one system.

## Core promise

Book the appointment, collect the payment, create the client record, and run the reminders automatically.

## Market position

Kujua Time sits between lightweight schedulers and heavier practice software. It is intended to be:

- more brandable than generic scheduling links
- stronger on payments, reminders, and client records than simple booking tools
- easier to adopt than compliance-heavy practice software
- Africa-friendly from day one, especially with Paystack-first support

## Launch category

Branded scheduling + payments + reminders + client CRM for service businesses.

## Primary customer segments

- coaches
- consultants
- therapists and counselors
- tutors and trainers
- beauty and wellness professionals
- photographers and creative professionals
- freelancers who book sessions or calls
- real estate professionals
- legal and financial advisors
- small agencies

## Core product lanes

### 1. Solo Scheduling
Branded booking page, event types, availability rules, calendar sync, reminders, payments, client records, intake forms, and notes.

### 2. Practice OS
Client portal, session notes, files, invoicing, packages, and recurring follow-up workflows.

### 3. Team Scheduling
Round robin, collective scheduling, routing forms, shared inbox, team roles, permissions, and analytics.

## Product moat

Kujua Time wins when every booking becomes an operational record.
